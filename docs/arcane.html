About Arcane (2021-2024)

Set in the divided world of Piltover and Zaun, Arcane explores the origins of League of Legends champions like Vi and Jinx. Directed by Pascal Charrue and Arnaud Delord, the 2021 animated series follows the two sisters caught in the tension between a city of progress and its oppressed underbelly. As science and magic collide, Arcane delves into themes of family, betrayal, and personal conflict, with stunning animation and rich character development. A thrilling blend of action, drama, and heart, Arcane brings the League of Legends universe to life in an unforgettable way.

Director: Pascal Charrue
